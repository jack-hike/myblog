    - title: "What are the types of point-of-sale system?"
      content: |
        There are many different types of point-of-sale system.

        Mobile POS systems have no fixed point — they can easily be moved around a store, salon floor or restaurant, and are accessible via your tablet or smartphone. Desktop point-of-sale systems run on a computer. These systems can be bulky. Self-serve kiosks — like those you’d see at Coles, Woolworths and McDonald’s — are another type of point-of-sale system.

        There are also specific point-of-sale systems for different types of businesses, such as hospitality point-of-sale systems, retail point-of-sale systems, beauty point-of-sale systems, and entertainment point-of-sale systems.

    - title: "Why do you need a POS system?"
