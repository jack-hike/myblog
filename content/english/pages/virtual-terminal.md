    - title: "What's the difference between a terminal and a Virtual Terminal?"
      content: |
       Payments made with NadaPay Virtual Terminal are safe and secure for your business, and your customers, for several reasons:
       - With NadaPay Virtual Terminal, you can offer customers the choice to enter their details from their own device using Payment Links. By sending your customer a secure SMS or email to input their own card details and complete the Virtual Terminal payment, your customer's card details are never exposed. 
       - NadaPay Virtual Terminal transactions take place through our secure payment gateway, that utilises data encryption or tokenization to secure sensitive information during transmission. 
       - Customer card details are not stored in your NadaPay Account, meaning sensitive data is never accessible to employees in your business.
       
    - title: "What's the difference between a MOTO payment and a Virtual Terminal payment?"
      content: |
       A MOTO (also known as mail order, telephone order) payment is processed by keying in a customer's card details directly into NadaPay EFTPOS Terminal. On the other hand, a Virtual Terminal payment is made without the need for an EFTPOS Terminal — by simply keying a customer's card details into a secure portal in your web browser.

       NadaPay Virtual Terminal is an ideal solution for businesses who don't have a high frequency of regular, in-person card payments, and therefore don't need the convenience of an EFTPOS Terminal — but still need a simple, low-cost way to accept card payments from customers.

    - title: "Why are Virtual Terminal payments priced differently?"
      content: "Virtual Terminal payments are another type of card not present (CNP) payments. A CNP payment is where a customer is not physically present to tap, insert, or swipe their card in a physical EFTPOS Terminal, so card details are shared online or over the phone. Transaction fees for payments made with NadaPay Virtual Terminal are priced differently to a payment made in-person with NadaPay Terminal due to the increased level of risk associated with CNP payments."
    - title: "Are there any subscription fees to use NadaPay Virtual Terminal?"
      content: "There are no monthly subscription fees to use NadaPay Virtual Terminal. You're only charged one low transaction fee of 1.7 + $0.25 (including GST) for all card types, for any payments accepted using NadaPay Virtual Terminal."
